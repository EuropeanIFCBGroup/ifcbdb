About IFCB

Imaging FlowCytobot (IFCB) is a submersible flow cytometer that takes images of phytoplankton. It is designed for continuous in situ operation and can be deployed for months at a time. It uses a combination of video and flow cytometric technology to both capture images of organisms for identification and measure chlorophyll fluorescence and light scattering associated with each image. The measurements of chlorophyll fluorescence enable users to more efficiently analyze phytoplankton cells by triggering on chlorophyll-containing particles.

IFCB is commercially available from McLane research laboratories.
